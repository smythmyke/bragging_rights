Privacy Policy for Bragging Rights

Effective Date: December 28, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bragging Rights ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, username, and profile photo
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens (via Firebase Auth)
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers (hashed) when you invite friends

Activity Data

  • Pool Participation: Your betting selections, pool history, and win/loss records
  • In-App Actions: Features you use, buttons you click, and pages you view
  • Transaction History: BR Coin purchases and usage

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App performance data, crash reports, and analytics (via Firebase Analytics)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our sports betting pool services
  • Process your BR Coin purchases and manage your virtual currency balance
  • Enable social features like friend invitations and head-to-head pools
  • Send notifications about pool updates and results
  • Improve and optimize our app performance
  • Ensure fair play and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information only in these circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your username, profile photo, and pool participation are visible to other users in your pools
  • Service Providers: With Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and analytics services
  • Payment Processors: With Google Play for in-app purchases
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase cloud services
  • All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols
  • Phone numbers are hashed before storage for privacy protection
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Update: Correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications in app settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email provided below.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 18 years of age due to simulated gambling content. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we discover such information has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using our app, you consent to such transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy
