Video Scale Comparison

Pixel 8a Screen (360x800 logical pixels) - Video Aspect Ratio 16:9

1. Contain (Letterbox)

Video maintains aspect ratio with black bars top/bottom

Specifications:
• Video Size: 360x202px (16:9)
• Black Bars: 175px each
• No distortion
• Full video visible
AspectRatio(
  aspectRatio: 16/9,
  child: VideoPlayer(_controller),
)
RECOMMENDED

2. Cover (Cropped Edges)

Video fills screen, edges cropped to fit

Specifications:
• Video Size: 1422x800px (scaled)
• Horizontal crop: ~531px each side
• No black bars
• Immersive experience
FittedBox(
  fit: BoxFit.cover,
  child: SizedBox(
    width: _controller.value.size.width,
    height: _controller.value.size.height,
    child: VideoPlayer(_controller),
  ),
)

3. Stretch to Fill

Video stretched to fill, causes distortion

BR
STRETCHED
Specifications:
• Video Size: 360x800px
• Vertical stretch: 396%
• Aspect ratio distorted
• Not recommended
Container(
  width: double.infinity,
  height: double.infinity,
  child: VideoPlayer(_controller),
)

4. Zoom to Fill (Centered)

Video zoomed 120% and centered

BR
ZOOMED 120%
Specifications:
• Video Size: 432x960px (120%)
• Overflow hidden
• Centered focus
• Slight crop all around
Transform.scale(
  scale: 1.2,
  child: Center(
    child: AspectRatio(
      aspectRatio: 16/9,
      child: VideoPlayer(_controller),
    ),
  ),
)

Recommendation: Use Option 2 (Cover)

For the BR Zoom animation, the Cover option provides the most immersive experience. The slight cropping of edges won't affect the main BR logo zoom effect, and it eliminates distracting black bars. The video will fill the entire screen for maximum impact.
